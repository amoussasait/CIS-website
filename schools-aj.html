Welcome to Akram Jomaa Islamic School

Akram Jomaa Islamic School is dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive education that integrates Alberta Education curriculum with authentic Islamic teachings. Our experienced educators create a nurturing environment where students develop academically, spiritually, and socially.

We are committed to excellence in all aspects of education, preparing students to be confident, knowledgeable, and principled individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

Our Programs

📚

Academics

Comprehensive curriculum aligned with Alberta Education standards, featuring Math, Science, Language Arts, Social Studies, and more. Small class sizes ensure personalized attention.

🕌

Islamic Studies

In-depth study of Islamic history, beliefs, jurisprudence, and character development. Students learn to apply Islamic principles in daily life.

📖

Quran & Arabic

Structured Quran memorization, recitation (Tajweed), and Arabic language instruction. Students progress at their own pace with qualified teachers.

⚽

Student Life

Extracurricular activities, sports, arts, field trips, and community service opportunities that develop well-rounded students.
