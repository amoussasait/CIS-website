Future Campus - Coming Soon

Planning is underway to expand Islamic education in Calgary

Growing to Serve Our Community

As Calgary's Muslim community continues to grow, CISS is committed to expanding access to excellent Islamic education. We are actively planning for a third school campus that will serve more families across the city.

Important: Planning is currently underway. We do not have confirmed dates or specific timelines at this time. Updates will be shared as plans develop.

Planning Phases

Phase 1: Community Needs Assessment

Understanding community needs, enrollment projections, and preferred locations across Calgary.

Phase 2: Site Selection & Acquisition

Identifying and securing an appropriate location that meets educational and community requirements.

Phase 3: Design & Planning

Architectural design, educational program planning, and regulatory approvals.

Phase 4: Construction & Preparation

Building construction, staff recruitment, curriculum development, and operational setup.

Phase 5: Opening

Grand opening and welcoming students to the new campus.

Vision for the New Campus

Our third campus will embody the same commitment to excellence that defines our existing schools, with modern facilities designed to support 21st-century learning:

  • State-of-the-art classrooms with flexible learning spaces
  • Dedicated Islamic studies and Quran learning centers
  • Science and technology labs
  • Multipurpose hall for assemblies and events
  • Library and resource center
  • Athletic facilities and outdoor play areas
  • Parent and community engagement spaces
