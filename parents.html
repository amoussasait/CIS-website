Frequently Asked Questions

School hours are typically 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Specific times may vary by campus and grade level. Please check with your school office for exact times.

Contact our admissions office at admissions@ciss-calgary.ca or call to schedule a campus tour. You'll receive an enrollment package with all required forms and information about the process.

We offer school bus service covering various areas of Calgary. Routes and schedules are finalized before each school year. Contact transport@ciss-calgary.ca for current route information.

Students are required to wear modest Islamic attire. Specific uniform requirements vary by campus and are outlined in the student handbook provided at enrollment.

Parent portal access is coming soon. Currently, report cards are distributed three times per year. You can also schedule meetings with teachers to discuss your child's progress.

Please keep your child home if they have a fever, are vomiting, or have contagious symptoms. Notify the school office by 9:00 AM on the day of absence.

Hot lunch programs vary by campus. Some schools offer weekly hot lunch options (pizza day, etc.). All food served is halal. Students may also bring lunch from home.

Join the parent council, volunteer for events, chaperone field trips, or contribute your skills and expertise. Contact your school office to learn about current opportunities.

Activities include sports teams, STEM club, art, debate, Quran competitions, and more. Offerings vary by campus and grade level. Check with your school for the current schedule.

Contact your child's teacher directly via email or call the school office to schedule a meeting. Regular parent-teacher conferences are held twice per year.
