Board of Directors

Dedicated leaders serving Calgary's Islamic education community with expertise and commitment

Governance & Leadership

The CISS Board of Directors provides strategic direction, governance oversight, and fiduciary responsibility for the Calgary Islamic School Society. Our board members bring diverse expertise from education, business, healthcare, technology, and community leadership to ensure excellent stewardship of our schools and resources.

Board members serve voluntarily, driven by a commitment to advancing Islamic education and supporting student success across Calgary.

Our Directors

Long-time community leader involved in establishing and expanding Islamic education in Calgary, focused on long-term growth and graduating future leaders.

Omar El-Hajjar

Chairman Operations

Vice President of Operations in the oil and gas sector. Involved with Calgary Islamic School and the Muslim Council of Calgary since 2015. Chairman of the Muslim Council of Calgary and a founding leader of CISS to ensure focused governance of Islamic schools.

Mohammed Abdel-Hafez

Vice Chair Healthcare

Vice Chair of CISS, serving since December 2023. Family physician and Assistant Professor in the Department of Family Medicine at the University of Calgary. Parent of students at Akram Jomaa Islamic School and advocate for academic excellence and student well-being.

Ali Moussa

Education Technology

Academic Chair of Software Development at SAIT. Background in software development and education, with experience mentoring learners and professionals moving into tech. Passionate about modern education, industry connections, and student success.

Marwa Ajram

Basir Saleh

Hoda Kilani

Career Coach Education

Dr Hoda Kilani CCDP ®, CPCC is a certified Career and Academic coach. Through intentional curriculum design and training, she empowers students and the adults around them to confidently navigate and activate what's next. As a global speaker, she champions the importance of career literacy and gifted education. Beyond speaking engagements, she advances her two callings through coaching, community partnerships, conference presentations, academic publications, and blogs. She also engages diverse audiences worldwide by hosting career conversations on YouTube, podcasts, and radio in both English and Arabic.

Board Committees

Our board operates through specialized committees that focus on key areas of governance and operations:

Finance Committee

Oversees financial planning, budgeting, audit processes, and ensures responsible stewardship of resources.

Governance Committee

Manages board recruitment, orientation, policy development, and ensures effective governance practices.

Parent Engagement Committee

Strengthens parent-school partnerships, communication channels, and family involvement in education.

Strategic Planning Committee

Guides long-term vision, expansion planning, and strategic initiatives for CISS growth.

Contact the Board

The board welcomes input from parents, community members, and stakeholders. For inquiries, suggestions, or to request a meeting with the board, please contact:

Email: board@ciss-calgary.ca

Board meetings are held regularly throughout the year. Meeting schedules and minutes are available to the community upon request.
