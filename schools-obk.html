Welcome to Omar Ibn Al-Khattab School

Omar Ibn Al-Khattab School is committed to excellence in Islamic education, combining rigorous academic standards with authentic Islamic teachings. Named after the renowned companion of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), our school embodies values of justice, knowledge, and leadership.

We provide a supportive, engaging environment where students thrive academically, grow spiritually, and develop the character and skills needed to excel in all aspects of life.

Our Programs

📚

Academics

Full Alberta Education curriculum with enhanced learning opportunities. Focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and academic excellence across all subjects.

🕌

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive Islamic education including Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, and character building. Students develop a strong Islamic identity and moral foundation.

📖

Quran & Arabic

Daily Quran recitation and memorization with certified instructors. Arabic language program from beginner to advanced levels.

⚽

Student Life

Rich extracurricular program including sports teams, STEM club, debate, arts, and community engagement opportunities.
